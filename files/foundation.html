Borngreen foundation

We will create a ‘BornGreen Foundation’ funded by 50% of the profit BornGreen generates. With this foundation we will collaborate with experts working with kids and the environment.

Together we will translate these environmental challenges into 3 packages, each focused on an age category. Making it easier for parents and childcare workers to excite the attention of the nature and environment on the level of the child. Ensuring the personal sustainable development of the child is a discovery and experience, making the person from an early age on aware of the challenges our Planet has.
