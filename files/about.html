Let’s get to know us!

Our company

BornGreen serves the planet in the first place. We are a social entrepreneurship startup and we believe, that there should be a tree for every newborn, worldwide. A BornGreen tree is more than a gift because half of the profit is invested in guiding young kids towards becoming a sustainable citizen.

Our goals

We are contributing to the 13th UN Sustainable Development Goal: ‘Take action to combat climate change’. Our goal is to improve education, awareness of climate change mitigation, adaptation and early warning. It’s important to do this on a local level because children have a small world perspective and “their city, is their world”.

Our value

The BornGreen tree will become a part of the child’s life, creates storytelling and a connection with nature. Through local projects we are contributing to the Sustainable Development Goals, helping to create a better future for the next generations.

Our collaborations

At the moment, BornGreen is making collaboration in different municipalities in Denmark. We can confirm that 12 municipalities in Denmark currently have a big interest in collaborating with BornGreen.

To help us plant, maintain and grow the trees, we have formed a partnership with Aarestrup plant school. Their knowledge benefits to understand which areas are protected by the Naturhistorisk museum, in terms of how we can plant.
